# portfolio risk review

Suggested cadence: every 4 hours  
Suggested project mode: worktree  
Suggested sandbox: read-heavy; avoid unattended write access unless you need it

$portfolio-risk-review

Review current positions and open orders.

Focus on:
- largest exposures
- correlated themes
- duplicated thesis risk
- stale resting orders
- markets nearing resolution
- orders that should probably be canceled or revised

Return a concise action-oriented risk review.
Do not cancel or place orders.
