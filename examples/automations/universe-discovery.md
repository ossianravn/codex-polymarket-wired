$bet-discovery

Refresh the active Polymarket market universe using the cheap discovery profile unless a successful run exists from the last 12 hours.

Report only material candidates:

1. Top 10 `best_research_candidates`.
2. Top 10 `clean_catalyst_bets`.
3. Top 10 `market_making_candidates`.
4. Top 10 `resolution_watch` names.
5. Top 10 `low_attention_modelable` names.

Do not place trades.
Do not preview trades.
Do not promote anything to a watchlist unless explicitly instructed.

For each candidate, include:

- title
- category group / structural type / horizon
- implied probability
- liquidity and spread
- why it surfaced, using reason codes
- next handoff skill

Archive the run if no candidate clears configured thresholds.
