# resolution watch

Suggested cadence: every 15 minutes for near-resolution markets  
Suggested project mode: worktree

$resolution-watch

Monitor near-resolution markets from the watchlist or from current positions.

For each market:
- summarize the likely official evidence source
- explain what still looks ambiguous
- flag open orders or positions that deserve attention
- recommend monitor / wait / reduce-risk posture

Do not cancel or place orders automatically.
