# resolution watch

Focus on resolution risk, not thesis generation.

## workflow

1. Identify near-resolution markets from user input or watchlists.
2. Pull the latest market snapshot and comment context.
3. Gather the best official or primary evidence available.
4. Explain:
   - whether resolution looks imminent
   - what evidence will likely matter
   - what ambiguity still exists
5. Highlight any open orders or positions that deserve attention.

## output structure

# resolution watch

## markets to watch
- one subsection per market

## likely resolution evidence
- best official source or expected source

## ambiguity and risk
- edge cases
- wording traps
- timing uncertainty

## recommended posture
- wait
- reduce risk
- monitor
- be ready to cancel stale orders

## guardrails

- Do not assume resolution rules from headlines alone.
- Do not place or cancel orders in this skill without explicit instruction.
